Admissions

Our Admission Process in Four Simple Steps

We've made joining the Dorvas family as seamless as possible. Here's everything you need to know to secure your child's place at our academy.

01

Obtain Application Form

Download our application form online or collect a physical copy from the school's administrative office during working hours.

02

Submit Completed Form

Return the fully completed form along with all required documents. Our admissions team will acknowledge receipt and schedule next steps.

03

Placement Assessment

Your child will undertake a friendly, non-threatening placement assessment to help us identify the most appropriate class level for them.

04

Receive & Accept Offer

Successful applicants receive an official offer of admission. Accept the offer, complete registration, and welcome to the Dorvas family!

Academic Programmes

Comprehensive Education at Every Stage

From crèche through sixth form, we provide a structured, enriching educational journey tailored to each developmental stage of your child's life.

Ages 0 – 5

Crèche & Nursery

A warm, stimulating early years environment that fosters language development, motor skills, social confidence, and a lifelong love of learning.

Ages 6 – 11

Basic (Primary) School

Core literacy, numeracy, science, and the arts delivered through a rich, multi-curriculum framework that builds strong academic foundations.

Ages 12 – 17

Secondary School

Rigorous preparation for WAEC, NECO, and IGCSE examinations, combined with co-curricular activities that shape future leaders.

Ages 17 – 19

Sixth Form

Advanced level study preparing students for university admission locally and internationally, with personal guidance and academic mentoring.
